The fifth rule of ARIA

The fifth rule is: all interactive elements must have an accessible name.

Native HTML elements usually have a built-in way to provide an accessible name - like the 
label
of a form input or the text content of a button or link.
Otherwise, the 
aria-label
or 
aria-labelledby
attribute must be used.
