Sometimes just HTML isn't enough

Many of the roles available to accessibility APIs have no corresponding native HTML element:

  • switch
  • tab
  • tree
  • and many many more
This was even worse before HTML5 became widespread (around 2015), as HTML5 introduced many new elements corresponding to particular roles - eg 
<nav>
for the 
navigation
role.
