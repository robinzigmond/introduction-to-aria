The fourth rule of ARIA

The fourth rule is: do not use 
role="presentation"
or 
aria-hidden="true"
on a focusable element .

In plain language, this says that any focusable element must be exposed in the accessibility tree. Otherwise assistive technology users would be unable to interact with elements that other users can.
